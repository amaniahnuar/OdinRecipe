Tamago Kake Gohan (TKG)

Description

Probably the simplest complete food. The name basically translates to "egg on rice". It's a simple dish originated from Japan, consist of two main Ingredients: rice and egg. Complete of the macronutrients needed by the body. Why did I choose this dish to be on the list?

- Because it's my poor man's comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • 1 fresh egg (preferably pasteurized for safety)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Optional toppings:
    • Chopped spring onions
    • Furikake (Japanese rice seasoning)
    • Nori (seaweed strips)
    • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook Japanese short-grain rice until hot and fluffy, then place 1 cup into a serving bowl.
  2. Crack a fresh egg directly onto the hot rice.
  3. Drizzle 1 teaspoon of soy sauce over the egg and rice.
  4. Stir the egg, soy sauce, and rice together vigorously until creamy and well-mixed.
  5. Add optional toppings like spring onions, furikake, nori strips, or sesame seeds for extra flavor.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your Tamago Kake Gohan!
